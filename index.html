About the Project

CRIMA focuses on the development of a decision-support system to enhance climate and environmental risk management. The system semantically integrates heterogeneous sources of information using a Virtual Knowledge Graph (VKG) and is powered by AI-supported querying of Big Earth Data.

It aims to help non-technical users query and interpret complex environmental data through natural language and conceptual models like impact chains. The system will be evaluated in two application scenarios in the Alpine region: one on hydrometeorological events, and one on water scarcity.



CRIMA is funded by the European Regional Development Fund (FESR).
