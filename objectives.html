Project Objectives

  • Develop a VKG that integrates quantitative Earth Observation (EO) data and scientific literature using semantic web technologies.
  • Enable AI-assisted querying and analysis of Big Earth Data.
  • Apply innovative conceptual models (e.g., impact chains) to describe climate risk components and their interrelations.
  • Use openEO APIs and extensions to support data access and processing.
  • Develop user-friendly interfaces including SPARQL and LLM/NLP querying.
  • Demonstrate the system in real-world application scenarios.

Challenges and Opportunities

  • Integrating multiple knowledge domains: environmental data, vulnerability, and adaptation measures.
  • Making a rich body of scientific knowledge accessible and usable by stakeholders.
  • Supporting non-technical users through intuitive interfaces and semantic tools.

Synergies

CRIMA contributes to climate change adaptation planning in South Tyrol and the broader Alpine region. It supports key SDG priorities: Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11), Climate Action (SDG 13), and Life on Land (SDG 15).
