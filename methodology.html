Research Questions

The research is based on three pillars: VKG data modeling, impact chain conceptualization, and integration with openEO.

  1. How can impact chains be formally modeled within VKGs?
  2. How can openEO processes be integrated into VKG queries, and which parts should be delegated?
  3. How can dynamic EO data be semantically linked to VKG instances of impact chain concepts?
