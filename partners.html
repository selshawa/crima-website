Partners

The CRIMA project is led by Eurac Research, and involves two research groups: the Advanced Computing for Earth Observation Research Group and the Climate and Disaster Risk Research Group. These teams at Eurac are responsible for coordinating project management, stakeholder engagement, development of openEO, and the application scenarios.

The KRDB Research Centre for Knowledge and Data of the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano leads the development of semantic models and the integration of openEO with the Virtual Knowledge Graph.

Ontopic, a local company in Bolzano, is responsible for implementing natural language and LLM-based interfaces for querying the system.
